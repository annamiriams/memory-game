Click on a card to reveal a bird on the other side. If you click on a second card and the birds match, you've found a pair. Oh, Flock!

A Wingspan-themed, single-player memory game.

Click on a card to reveal a bird on the other side. If you click on a second card and the birds match, you've found a pair. If the two birds don't match, the cards will flip back over, so do your best to remember their placement. Find all bird pairs on the board before you've used up all of your turns, and you've won!
